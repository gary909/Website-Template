SYNTHESIZER & PCB DESIGN

Other Hobbies...

As well as VR Development I also have a deep passion for designing and creating synthesisers (electronic musical instruments).

The majority of these instruments are built using C++, the Mozzi audio library, low cost Arduino development boards, and analog electronic circuits. The designs of which I make open source (available via my blog) and also publish instructions of how to make them for beginners.

If I make a small run of units, I'll often create PCBs using the Kicad software.

You can see some video examples here, here, and here.
