DIGITAL SUNSET STUDIOS


Purely a passion project, Digital Sunset Studios is the name I work under for my software projects.

In 2013 I first started playing with electronics, then later on after winning a Google programming scholarship, I started to combine this with software programming.

After taking an intensive 9 week Web Dev bootcamp in 2019 I have worked as a full time web developer, but at night I put on my glasses and become a VR Developer, as well as creating hardware synthersizers for fun.

I currently live in Leipzig, Germany.
